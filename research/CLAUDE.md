# research/ -- Agent Entry Point

This directory holds research tracks that inform harmonik's design but are not themselves spec artifacts. Each track has its own subfolder with its own `CLAUDE.md`, `METHODOLOGY.md`, and `STATUS.md`.

Research tracks are *not* kerf works. Kerf is for spec writing. Research here is exploratory and empirical: we are trying to discover patterns and frame questions, not to produce normative specs. Findings may later feed into a kerf work.

## Active tracks

- [`planning-protocols/`](planning-protocols/) -- Reusable shapes of human-agent interaction during idea -> plan -> spec work. Currently in Phase 1 (scoping).

## Rules for agents entering any track

- Read that track's `CLAUDE.md` and `STATUS.md` before doing anything.
- Do not merge tracks. Each track has its own lifecycle and artifacts.
- Do not put per-track content in this directory. Everything track-specific lives in its subfolder.
- Do not start a new track without user sign-off.
